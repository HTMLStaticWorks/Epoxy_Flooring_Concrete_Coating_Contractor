FAQ

Pricing Questions

Common questions about our pricing and what's included.

Speak to a Rep
What's included in the price?
All our pricing includes surface preparation (diamond grinding), crack repair (standard cracks), primer coat, base coat, decorative elements if applicable, and topcoat. Travel within our service area is also included. There are no hidden fees.
Do you charge for the initial consultation?
No. Our on-site evaluations are completely free and carry no obligation. We'll assess your floor, discuss your options, and provide a detailed written quote at no cost.
Can I get a price match?
Yes! If you receive a comparable written quote from another licensed, insured epoxy contractor, we'll beat their price by 5%. The comparison must be apples-to-apples — same materials, same preparation process, same warranty.
Are there financing options?
We partner with several financing companies to offer flexible payment plans for residential projects. Commercial projects can often be structured with progress billing. Ask us about options during your consultation.
What factors affect the final price?
Key factors include: total square footage, existing floor condition (heavy cracking/damage costs more to repair), coating system chosen (decorative vs. solid), anti-slip additive, color choice, and site access considerations.
