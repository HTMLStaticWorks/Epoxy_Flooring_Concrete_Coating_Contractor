Our Story

Built on Expertise & Integrity

Founded in 2009, EpoxyPro started with a simple mission: deliver floor coating solutions that don't just look great — they perform exceptionally for decades.

What began as a small garage floor operation in Houston, TX has grown into one of the region's most trusted flooring contractors, serving everything from luxury residences to 500,000+ sq ft industrial facilities. Our growth has been built entirely on referrals and repeat clients.

We combine cutting-edge coating technologies with old-fashioned craftsmanship values: meticulous preparation, premium materials, and relentless attention to detail on every single project.
