How We Work

Our 5-Step Process

A meticulous, step-by-step installation method designed for commercial-grade longevity.

01

Consultation

On-site moisture testing, concrete assessment, profiling review, and a fixed upfront quote.

02

Surface Prep

Industrial diamond grinding, vacuum dust collection, crack filling, and profiling for maximum bonding.

03

Coating Application

Our certified installers apply base primer, full broadcast flake/pigment, and polyaspartic coats.

04

Quality Inspection

Strict inspection of coat thickness, texture consistency, surface hardness, and edge sealing.

05

Project Completion

Final site cleanup, customer walkthrough review, and delivery of our comprehensive 5-year warranty.
