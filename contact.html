FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your free consultation.

How long does an epoxy floor installation take?
Most residential garage floors are completed in 1–2 days, including surface preparation and coating application. Commercial and industrial projects vary based on square footage and complexity.
How long before I can walk on my new floor?
Light foot traffic is typically safe within 12–24 hours. For vehicles and heavy equipment, we recommend waiting 72 hours. Polyaspartic coatings cure much faster — often within hours.
Do you offer a warranty on your work?
Yes! All our epoxy floor installations come with a comprehensive 5-year workmanship warranty. We also provide guidance on proper care to maximize your floor's lifespan.
How much does epoxy flooring cost?
Pricing varies based on the size of the area, surface condition, coating system, and decorative options chosen. Typical residential garage floors range from $3–$7 per sq ft. Contact us for a free, accurate quote.
Can epoxy be installed over existing coatings?
In most cases, no. Existing coatings are removed via diamond grinding to ensure proper adhesion. This prep work is critical to the longevity of your new floor system.
