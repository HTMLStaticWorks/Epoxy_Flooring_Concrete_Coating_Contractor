About This Service

Transform Your Garage into a Showroom

Our garage floor epoxy coating system is designed to completely transform your concrete slab into a beautiful, durable surface that will stand up to daily use, chemical spills, heavy vehicles, and the test of time. We use only commercial-grade, 100% solids epoxy materials — not the thin, water-based coatings you'll find at hardware stores.

Every garage floor coating project begins with our proprietary Diamond Prep™ surface preparation process, ensuring the epoxy bonds at a molecular level to your concrete — not just sitting on top. This is the single most important factor in coating longevity, and it's where most DIY and lesser contractors fall short.

Our Installation Process

01
Surface Grinding

Diamond grinding to open the concrete pores for maximum adhesion.

02
Crack Repair

Polyurea injection fills cracks and prevents future cracking through the coating.

03
Primer Application

Penetrating epoxy primer that chemically bonds to the concrete substrate.

04
Base Coat + Flakes

100% solids epoxy base coat with full-broadcast decorative vinyl flakes.

05
Flake Removal

Excess flakes scraped and collected for a smooth, uniform surface.

06
Polyaspartic Topcoat

UV-stable, high-gloss topcoat for lasting protection and shine.

Materials We Use

  • 100% solids epoxy (no solvents, no odor)
  • Commercial-grade vinyl flake chips
  • Aliphatic polyaspartic UV-stable topcoat
  • Polyurea crack filler
  • Optional non-slip aggregate additive

Talk to an Expert
How long does a garage floor coating take to install?
Most standard 2-car garages can be completed in a single day. We typically begin at 7am and are done by early afternoon, leaving your floor curing overnight for a 24-hour return-to-service.
How long before I can park on it?
Light foot traffic within 24 hours. Vehicles can park on it after 72 hours. Full chemical cure takes approximately 7 days, but you can use the floor normally long before that.
Can you coat a floor with existing cracks or damage?
Absolutely. We repair most cracks and damage as part of our standard surface preparation process using polyurea crack fillers and grinding. Severely damaged slabs may require additional work, which we'll assess during the free evaluation.
What is the warranty on your garage floors?
All our garage floor coatings come with a 5-year workmanship warranty. This covers peeling, bubbling, delamination, and any other defects caused by our installation. Material warranties vary by product used.
How do I clean and maintain my epoxy floor?
Simply sweep regularly and mop with a mild pH-neutral cleaner. Avoid harsh chemicals like bleach or ammonia. Our topcoat is chemical-resistant and easy to clean — spills can simply be wiped up.
