Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about epoxy floor installation, warranties, or cost? Find quick answers here.

Ask a Specialist
How long does the installation process take?
Most residential garage floor projects are completed in 1 to 2 days. Industrial and commercial projects depend on the square footage, but same-day curing polyaspartic systems are available to minimize business downtime.
What is the difference between standard epoxy and polyaspartic?
Polyaspartic coatings cure much faster (same-day turnaround), have higher UV stability (won't yellow in sunlight), and offer superior abrasion and chemical resistance compared to traditional retail-grade epoxy.
How do you prepare the concrete surface before coating?
Preparation is the most critical step. We use professional diamond grinders with industrial vacuums to grind the top layer of concrete, open up pores, repair all cracks/spalls, and create the ideal profile for coating adhesion.
Does the flooring come with a warranty?
Yes, all residential installations come with a comprehensive 5-year warranty against peeling, delamination, and workmanship defects. Commercial/industrial packages offer customized warranty protections.
Is the coated surface slippery when wet?
We offer slip-resistant additives (such as anti-slip aggregates or full-broadcast flakes) that create a textured, slip-resistant finish. This is highly recommended for garages, shops, showrooms, and wet commercial areas.
